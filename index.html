Simple Cooking Instructions

Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant. Add diced onions and cook until translucent. Season with salt and add tomatoes, simmer gently. Add basil and sugar to balance acidity. Bring salted water to boil for pasta. Cook pasta until al dente. Toss with sauce and serve with Parmesan cheese.

Complete Lasagna Instructions

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). This temperature ensures even cooking throughout. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage and ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Add minced onion and crushed garlic to the meat mixture. Cook for 3-4 minutes until fragrant. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Mix well to combine evenly. Add water, white sugar, dried basil leaves, fennel seeds, and Italian seasoning to the sauce. Season with 1 teaspoon of salt and black pepper. Add chopped fresh parsley and mix. Reduce heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil for the lasagna noodles. Cook lasagna noodles for 8-10 minutes until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with the egg. Mix well until smooth and creamy. Add the remaining 1/2 teaspoon of salt to the ricotta mixture and mix thoroughly. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray to prevent sticking. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of the prepared baking dish evenly. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce, overlapping slightly if necessary. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles using a spatula for even distribution. Top with a layer of mozzarella cheese slices. Repeat layers and finish with remaining sauce and cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
